# Stir Fry Receipe
Ingredients:
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1/2 onion, sliced
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1 carrot
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a pan.
2. Add garlic and onion, stir for 1 min.
3. Add remaining veggies, stir-fry for 3-4 mins.
4. Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
5. Serve hot!
